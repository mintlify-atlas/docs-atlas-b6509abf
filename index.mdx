## Overview

UCC Control de Acceso is a comprehensive access control system designed specifically for Universidad Cooperativa de Colombia. The system manages contingent entry for users who do not have their physical ID card, tracking failures, enforcing blocking rules, and providing administrators with real-time insights and reporting capabilities.
