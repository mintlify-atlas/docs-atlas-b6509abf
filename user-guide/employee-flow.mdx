# Employee User Flow

This guide covers the process for **UCC employees** who need contingent entry without their physical TIC card.

## Employee vs Student

<Note>
  The entry process is **identical** for employees and students. The main differences are:
  - Employee information displayed (cargo, dependencia)
  - Same 4-strike rule applies
  - Same blocking mechanisms
</Note>

## Complete Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Arrive at Campus">
    Employee arrives at UCC campus entrance without TIC card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Institutional ID">
    Enter your employee ID number (format: 80XXXXXX).
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Employee Profile">
    System displays:
    ```
    💼 María López García
    ID: 80234567 | CC: 9876543210
    
    EMPLEADO
    • Cargo: Coordinadora
    • Dependencia: Tesorería
    
    Fallas: 1/4
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register Entry">
    Click "Registrar Ingreso Sin Carnet" and select reason (Olvido/Perdida).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter or Visit Admin">
    Based on result, either enter campus or go to administration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multi-Role Employees

If you're both employee AND student:

```
👤 Carlos Mendoza
ID: 80999888

EMPLEADO
• Cargo: Asistente
• Dependencia: Biblioteca

ESTUDIANTE
• Programa: Maestría en Educación

Fallas: 2/4
```

<Warning>
  Failure counter applies to **your account**, not individual roles. Blocking affects all your roles.
</Warning>
