# Contractor User Flow

This guide explains the contingent entry process for **external contractors** working at UCC facilities.

## Who Are Contractors?

Contractors (Contratistas) are:
- External service providers
- Third-party company employees
- Temporary project workers
- Maintenance/support personnel

## Entry Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Present at Entrance">
    Arrive at designated contractor entrance without TIC.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide ID">
    Security guard enters your institutional ID.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Profile">
    ```
    🛠️ Pedro Rodríguez
    ID: 80888777 | CC: 5555555555
    
    CONTRATISTA
    • Empresa: ACME Services SAS
    
    Fallas: 0/4
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register Entry">
    Same process as students/employees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Notes

<Warning>
  Contractors are subject to the same rules:
  - 4-strike limit for "olvido"
  - Immediate block for "perdida"
  - Must visit administration when blocked
</Warning>

<Info>
  Your contract manager will be notified if you are blocked.
</Info>
